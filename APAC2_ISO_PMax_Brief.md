**Prepared:** June 9, 2026

---

## The Short Version

The campaign dropped from **~18 conversions/month at $14K CPA** to **5 conversions/month at $78K CPA**. The trigger was a single asset group swap in March 2026 — the group that drove all results was paused and replaced with one that uses AI-generated images and weaker copy. Spend kept running at full pace while performance collapsed.

## Root Cause

### Asset group swap — March 2026

Two asset groups have run on this campaign:

**"APAC | ISO | Generic Assets"** — now PAUSED
- Active: Jul 2025 → Mar 2026
- Lifetime conversions: **91**
- Drove 100% of the campaign's historical results

**"Asset Group 1"** — currently ENABLED
- Active: Mar 2026 → now
- Lifetime conversions: **17**
- All images are AI-generated (created March 18, 2026)

The old group was paused in March 2026. The replacement group has not come close to replicating its performance despite running for 3 months and spending over $800K.

---

### What specifically changed

**Copy quality degraded**

The old group ran benefit-driven, specific headlines that got people to click:

| Headline | Conv | CTR |
|----------|------|-----|
| "Budget-friendly ISO 27001" | 12.9 | 0.6% |
| "Get ISO Compliant in 2 weeks" | 12.1 | **8.7%** |
| "ISO 27001 for SaaS Companies" | 8.2 | 2.0% |
| "Accelerate your security audit" | 8.0 | **14.4%** |
| "ISO Certification in clicks" | 6.0 | 6.2% |
| "ISO 27001, Simplified & Fast" | 5.8 | 7.1% |
| "Crush ISO 27001 Faster" | 4.0 | **7.6%** |

The new group runs generic, category-level headlines:

| Headline | Conv | CTR |
|----------|------|-----|
| "ISO 27001 Automated" | 7.0 | 1.0% |
| "ISO/IEC 27001" | 4.0 | 3.0% |
| "ISO 27001 Certification" | 1.0 | 1.3% |
| "ISO 27001 Certification Cost" | 1.0 | 1.4% |
| "ISO 27001 for SaaS" | 1.0 | 2.1% |

**CTR dropped from a 7-14% range to 1-3%.** Lower CTR signals lower relevance to Google's auction system, which directly drops ad quality scores and reduces impression share.

**Images are AI-generated**

Every image in the current active group has the name "Generated image — 2026-03-18". None of these existed before March 2026. The old group had 34+ real images built over time. AI-generated images with no track record start with no quality signal — Google's system needs time to learn which images work, but the current creative quality is not producing that signal.

**Google quality score deteriorated**

The Rank Lost Impression Share (lost due to quality, not budget) has been:
- Nov–Dec 2025: 59–63%
- Feb–Mar 2026: 69–70%
- Apr–May 2026: 67–72%

Even when the campaign has enough budget, it is losing auctions because Google ranks the ads lower. This is a direct consequence of weaker creative.

## What Needs to Happen

**1. Re-enable "APAC | ISO | Generic Assets"** *(fastest fix)*
The group with 91 lifetime conversions is sitting paused. Re-enabling it should begin recovering performance within 1–2 weeks. Do not pause "Asset Group 1" — run both and let Google allocate.

**2. Move the proven copy into "Asset Group 1"**
Add these headlines back immediately:
- "Get ISO Compliant in 2 weeks"
- "Accelerate your security audit"
- "Budget-friendly ISO 27001"
- "ISO Certification in clicks"
- "Crush ISO 27001 Faster"

**3. Replace AI-generated images with real creative**
All current images are AI-generated with zero history. Add the original real images from "APAC | ISO | Generic Assets" into "Asset Group 1". Real product screenshots, customer results, or team imagery consistently outperform AI-generated stock in PMax for B2B SaaS.

**4. Do not change bids or budgets until creative is fixed**
The problem is not spend level — it is ad quality. Increasing budget into a low-quality creative pool just burns more money at a bad CPA. Budget efficiency will recover on its own once quality recovers.
